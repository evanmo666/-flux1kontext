Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Flux Kontext different from other AI image generators?

+

Flux Kontext combines cutting-edge AI technology with intuitive controls, offering superior image quality, faster processing times, and more precise control over the generation process. Our contextual understanding allows for more accurate interpretation of complex prompts.

How fast can I generate images?

+

Most images are generated within 5-15 seconds, depending on complexity and resolution. Our optimized infrastructure ensures rapid processing without compromising on quality.

What image resolutions and formats are supported?

+

We support multiple resolutions from 512x512 up to 4K (4096x4096) and various formats including PNG, JPEG, and WebP. Higher resolutions are available for professional users.

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and do not store your prompts or generated images unless you choose to save them. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Yes, all images generated with Flux Kontext can be used for both personal and commercial purposes. You retain full ownership and rights to your creations.
